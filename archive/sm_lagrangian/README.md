# Archived internal working draft

This folder contains an early internal working draft on the sector-by-sector projection of the discrete octonion action onto the Standard Model Lagrangian.

The material was later consolidated (together with the companion TUFT/Hopf mass-spectrum draft) into the single combined paper at `../tuft_sm_lagrangian/`.

These drafts were never released, preprinted, or deposited. They are kept here purely for historical reference during development. No citation keys were ever assigned to them.

See the combined paper for the final integrated treatment.
