# Archived internal working draft

This folder contains an early internal working draft on the TUFT/Hopf topological necessity arguments and the dynamic inner--outer Casimir mass spectrum on the octonion carrier.

The material was later consolidated (together with the companion Lagrangian sector-projection draft) into the single combined paper at `../tuft_sm_lagrangian/`.

These drafts were never released, preprinted, or deposited. They are kept here purely for historical reference during development. No citation keys were ever assigned to them.

See the combined paper for the final integrated treatment.
